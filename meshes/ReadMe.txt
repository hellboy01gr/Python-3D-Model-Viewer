[HOW TO MAKE 3D MODELS FOR THIS PROGRAM]

Step 1. Open a 3d modelling program of you choice (I am using Blender for this guide)
Step 2. Make you 3d model (keep in mind -Y is the front of your model)
Step 3. Go to File >> Export >> Wavefront (.obj) and export preferably in the program's meshes folder
Step 4. In the program go to Settings >> Select model... And load your model
